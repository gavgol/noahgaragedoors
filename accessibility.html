Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Noah Garage Doors wants every customer in San Diego to be able to use this website, including people who use screen readers, keyboard navigation, screen magnification, or other assistive technology. Accessibility is something we work on continuously, not a box we check once.

Our goal

We aim to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, the standard referenced under the Americans with Disabilities Act and California's Unruh Civil Rights Act. We are not claiming the site is perfect. Some pages may still fall short in places, and we treat any barrier you find as a problem to fix.

What we do

  • Build pages with real headings, labels, and alt text rather than relying on an automatic "overlay" plugin to paper over problems.
  • Keep text and background colors at readable contrast and let the page work with the keyboard alone.
  • Offer on-page tools (the accessibility button in the corner of every page) to enlarge text, raise contrast, highlight links, switch to a plainer font, and pause animations. These are conveniences layered on top of an accessible page, not a substitute for one.
  • Review and correct accessibility issues as we become aware of them.

Third-party content

Some parts of the site rely on outside services, such as embedded Google Maps. We do not control how those third parties build their tools, and their accessibility may vary. If one of them blocks you, contact us and we will get you the same information or service another way.

Tell us about a barrier

If any part of this website is hard or impossible for you to use, please tell us. Describe the page and the problem, and we will do our best to fix it and to give you the information, product, or service you needed through an accessible method in the meantime.
