When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, phone number, requested service, message, and basic attribution information such as the page or campaign that brought you to us. We use this information to respond to and manage your service inquiry.

Your contact information is used only to follow up on your garage door service request. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Our contact form sends your inquiry to our field-service CRM so we can follow up, schedule work, and maintain a service history. Authorized service staff may access this information. We retain lead and service records for legitimate business, accounting, and customer-service purposes.
