Common Garage Door Problems We Fix in Poway

Garage doors are complex systems, and various issues can arise over time. In Poway, factors like consistent sunlight exposure and temperature swings can sometimes accelerate wear. Here are some of the most common problems we address:

  • Broken Garage Door Springs: These are under immense tension and can snap without warning. A broken spring will prevent your door from opening manually or with the opener. Our manufacturer's spring warranty covers this critical repair.
  • Garage Door Opener Malfunctions: Whether your opener isn't responding, making strange noises, or the door only moves partially, we can repair or replace all types of openers, including chain-drive, belt-drive, and screw-drive systems.
  • Frayed or Snapped Cables: Cables work in conjunction with springs to lift and lower the door. If they fray or snap, the door can become uneven or entirely inoperable.
  • Worn Out Rollers: Noisy or grinding rollers indicate wear. Replacing them can significantly improve door operation, making it smoother and quieter.
  • Misaligned or Bent Tracks: If your garage door runs off its tracks or struggles to move, the tracks may be bent or misaligned. This requires precise adjustment to prevent further damage.
  • Damaged Panels: Accidents happen, leading to dents or cracks in garage door panels. We can often replace individual panels, restoring the door's appearance and structural integrity.
  • Sensor Problems: Safety sensors prevent the door from closing on obstructions. If they are blocked or misaligned, the door won't close properly.
