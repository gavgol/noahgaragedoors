1. Program Description

Noah Garage Doors offers a text messaging program that sends garage door service reminders, appointment updates, and occasional promotional offers to customers who have given us their mobile number and agreed to receive texts. By providing your mobile number and opting in, you agree to these terms.

2. How to Opt In

You opt in by giving us your mobile number and agreeing to receive text messages when you call us for service, when we come out to your home, or when you submit the contact form on this website. We only text customers who have provided their number to us.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You will typically receive occasional service reminders and offers, not a fixed number of messages.

4. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Charges depend on your mobile carrier and plan. Noah Garage Doors is not responsible for any charges from your carrier.

5. How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message at any time to opt out. After you reply STOP, you will receive one confirmation message and no further texts. You can opt back in at any time by replying START.

6. How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any message for help, or contact us directly at (619) 572-4266 or Noahgaragedoors@gmail.com.

7. Privacy

No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
