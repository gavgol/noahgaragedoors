When to Call an Emergency Garage Door Service

Not every garage door problem needs emergency service — but these situations absolutely do:

🔓

Door Stuck Open

Your home is completely exposed. This is a security emergency — don't wait until morning. We can often respond within 1–2 hours.

🚗

Car Trapped Inside

Spring broke, door went off-track, or opener failed with your vehicle inside. We get you out, same day.

💥

Loud Bang / Spring Snapped

If you heard a loud bang from the garage, your spring likely failed. Do not operate the door — call us.

🔧

Door Off Its Tracks

A door off-track can fall without warning. Stop operating it immediately and call. This is a safety hazard.

📿

Cables Snapped

Broken cables put the full weight on the door without control. The door can drop suddenly. Do not use it.

🚨

Panel Damaged / Bent Inward

Impact from a vehicle or storm can compromise the door structure and jam it in place. We assess and repair.
