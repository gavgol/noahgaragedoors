## 2. The winning formula (repeats across all top ads)

1. **Pain-question headline** → "Got a broken garage door?" / "Garage door problems?" / "Is your garage door having issues?"
2. **Urgency** → "Same-Day" / "Around the clock" (in 4 of 5 ads)
3. **Offer that lowers the barrier** → Free estimate / $ off repair / Save up to $X
4. **Direct CTA button** → "Book Now" / "Get Free Quote" (NOT "Learn More")
5. **Visual that proves the work** → Before/After grid (best for a new business) or branded service van

## 3. Creative direction for Noah (recommended)

- **Format:** Before/After — the single most proven garage-door creative. Builds trust through *work*, not claims.
- **Use REAL photos** (already on hand — see assets). Do NOT use AI-generated doors.
- Bold, high-contrast, mobile-first. Big readable text, badges on images, one clear CTA.
- Make 2 sizes: **1080×1080** (feed) and **1080×1350** (portrait, better reach).
